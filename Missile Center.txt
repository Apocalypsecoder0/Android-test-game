Here different nukes can be built and created. Everything needs to be configured for these

For this following has to be configurable:
[*]Name
[*]Damage in % to land, pop, military
[*]Max damage for land
[*]Cost in Money
[*]Cost in Power
[*]Time to build in hours

You can only ever have one of each type of missile and can produce one if you already have one.
